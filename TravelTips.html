There are many places to visit in Penang. But you can’t travel to every place in one day.

Penang offers countless attractions, but exploring them all in one day is impossible—plan wisely to make the most of your visit.

Package one: 2 days, 1 night

Morning:
• Breakfast: Enjoy a traditional Penang breakfast of roti canai or nasi lemak.
• Street Art Tour: Discover the vibrant street art in George Town,
particularly along Armenian Street and Ah Quee Street.

Late morning:
• Pinang Peranakan Mansion: Dive into the rich Peranakan culture with a guided tour of this heritage mansion.
• Little India: Explore the colorful streets, shop for spices, and enjoy a traditional Indian lunch.

Afternoon:
• Penang Hill: Take the funicular to the top for breathtaking island views and a relaxing tea break.
• Lunch: Char Kway Teow: Stir-fried flat rice noodles with prawns, Chinese sausage, and bean sprouts in a savory sauce.

Evening:
• Gurney Drive: Stroll along the seafront and indulge in local hawker favorites like char kway teow and asam laksa.

Night:
• Chulia Street Night Market: Enjoy street food and shop for souvenirs before returning to your hotel.

Day 2: Nature and Heritage

Morning:
• Penang Botanical Gardens: Start your day with a serene walk through lush gardens.
• Cheong Fatt Tze Mansion (The Blue Mansion): Explore this famous heritage building and learn about its historical significance.

Late morning:
• Clan Jetties: Visit the Chew Jetty to experience the unique stilted houses and traditional Chinese clan life.

Afternoon:
• Lunch in George Town: Savor Penang curry mee or wantan mee at a local eatery.
• Shopping on Penang Road: Pick up local products like nutmeg oil and Penang white coffee as souvenirs.

Depature:
• Hotel Check-Out: Prepare for departure and transfer to Penang International Airport.

Package two: 2 days, 1 night

Morning:
• Arrival in Penang: Check into your hotel in Batu Ferringhi.
• Breakfast: Begin with a beachfront breakfast at a local café, enjoying the coastal views.
• George Town Heritage Walk: Head to George Town to explore its UNESCO World Heritage sites, including Fort Cornwallis and the Esplanade.

Late morning:
• Khoo Kongsi Clan House: Discover this beautifully preserved Chinese clan house, rich with intricate carvings and history.
• Penang State Museum: Learn about Penang’s history and culture through fascinating exhibits.

Afternoon:
• Lunch at a Heritage Café: Enjoy a meal at a café housed in a historic building, savoring local dishes like nasi kandar.
• Cheong Fatt Tze Mansion (The Blue Mansion): Take a guided tour of this iconic blue mansion, famous for its unique architecture.

Evening:
• Batu Ferringhi Beach: Return to Batu Ferringhi for a relaxing evening on the beach. Enjoy water sports or simply unwind by the shore.
• Night Market: Explore the Batu Ferringhi Night Market for local crafts, souvenirs, and street food.

Night:
• Beachfront Dinner: Dine at a seaside restaurant, indulging in fresh seafood while watching the sunset.

Day 2: Nature and Adventure

Morning:
• Penang National Park: Start your day with a hike in Penang National Park. Choose between trails leading to the famous Monkey Beach or the historic lighthouse at Muka Head.
• Canopy Walk: Experience the Canopy Walk within the park for a unique perspective of the rainforest.

Late morning:
• Entopia by Penang Butterfly Farm: Visit this interactive butterfly sanctuary and learn about various species of butterflies and insects.

Afternoon:
• Lunch at Teluk Bahang: Enjoy a local meal at a restaurant near the park, sampling dishes like ikan bakar (grilled fish) or mee udang (prawn noodles).
• Tropical Spice Garden: Explore this lush garden showcasing a wide variety of tropical plants and spices. Take a guided tour to learn about their culinary and medicinal uses.

Departure:
• Hotel Check-Out: Head back to your hotel to check out.

Package three: 3 Days, 2 Nights

Day 1: Arrival and Urban Exploration

Morning:
• Arrival in Penang: Check into your hotel in George Town.
• Breakfast: Enjoy a traditional Penang breakfast of roti bakar (toasted bread with kaya) and kopi at a local kopitiam.
• Street Art and Heritage Walk: Start your day exploring George Town's vibrant street art and heritage sites, including the Clan Jetties and Armenian Street.

Late morning:
• Pinang Peranakan Mansion: Visit this cultural gem to learn about the Peranakan community's rich history and lifestyle.
• Lunch at Little India: Savor a flavorful Indian meal, such as banana leaf rice or tandoori chicken, in Little India.

Afternoon:
• Penang State Museum: Immerse yourself in the history and culture of Penang through the museum's well-curated exhibits.
• Cheong Fatt Tze Mansion (The Blue Mansion): Take a guided tour of this architectural masterpiece to discover the life and legacy of Cheong Fatt Tze.

Evening:
• Gurney Plaza: If time permits, visit Gurney Plaza for some shopping or a movie before returning to your hotel.

Day 2: Nature and Cultural Exploration

Morning:
• Penang Hill: Start your day with a trip to Penang Hill via the funicular train. Enjoy panoramic views of the island and explore the hilltop attractions, including The Habitat's nature trails and canopy walk.
• Breakfast on Penang Hill: Have breakfast at one of the hilltop cafes, taking in the cool breeze and scenic vistas.

Late morning:
• Kek Lok Si Temple: Visit this sprawling temple complex in Air Itam, home to the impressive statue of the Goddess of Mercy and the Pagoda of Ten Thousand Buddhas.

Afternoon:
• Lunch at Ayer Itam Market: Enjoy a hearty bowl of Penang laksa at the famous Ayer Itam Market.
• Tropical Spice Garden: Explore this lush garden in Teluk Bahang, where you can learn about tropical plants and spices. Consider joining a cooking class to prepare local dishes using fresh ingredients.

Evening:
• Batu Ferringhi Beach: Spend a relaxing evening at Batu Ferringhi, where you can partake in water sports, relax on the beach, or visit the Batu Ferringhi Night Market for local crafts and souvenirs.
• Beachfront Dinner: Dine at a seaside restaurant, enjoying fresh seafood while watching the sunset.

Day 3: Adventure and Relaxation

Morning:
• Penang National Park: Begin your day with an adventurous hike in Penang National Park. Choose a trail leading to the picturesque Monkey Beach or the lighthouse at Muka Head.
• Canopy Walk: Experience the Canopy Walk in the park, offering a unique view of the rainforest from above.

Late morning:
• Entopia by Penang Butterfly Farm: Visit this interactive butterfly sanctuary and explore its lush gardens, filled with butterflies and other insects.

Afternoon:
• Lunch at Teluk Bahang: Enjoy a local meal, perhaps sampling ikan bakar (grilled fish) or mee goreng at a nearby restaurant.
• Explore Balik Pulau: Take a scenic drive to Balik Pulau, known for its tranquil countryside, durian farms, and picturesque landscapes. Stop by a local farm to taste fresh durian or try other local fruits.

Evening:
• Return to George Town: Head back to George Town for a final stroll through the historic streets, enjoying the evening atmosphere.
• Farewell Dinner: Conclude your trip with a special dinner at a heritage restaurant in George Town, sampling more of Penang's famous dishes.

Night:
• Hotel Check-Out: Return to your hotel to check out.
