There are a lot of good restaurants all over Penang!

Penang boasts a plethora of excellent restaurants offering diverse and delicious cuisine.

1. Heritage Artisans

Experience the rich flavors of Peranakan cuisine crafted by our passionate Baba and Nyonya artisans. Guided by Baba Jerry, savor traditional recipes with creative presentations and wine pairings. Reservations only; no walk-ins.

Price range: RM 349.00 – RM 499.00

Cuisines: Chinese, Asian, Contemporary, Nonya/Malaysian, Malaysian

2. Two Frenchies Cafe Bistrot

Two Frenchies Cafe Bistrot offers authentic French cuisine in a charming, relaxed setting. Located in George Town, Penang, it is known for its diverse menu featuring classic French dishes and creative presentations. The bistro is perfect for both casual meals and special occasions, offering breakfast, lunch, and dinner with options for private events. The pet-friendly and wheelchair-accessible venue ensures a welcoming atmosphere for all guests.

Cuisines: French, European

3. Orinea Restaurant

They culinary experts transform traditional dishes using the finest ingredients for an unforgettable experience. Enjoy exceptional food, service, and ambiance for a remarkable fine dining experience.

Price range: RM 86.00 – RM 785.00

Cuisines: Contemporary

4. Kebaya Dining Room

Kebaya is the dining room of the Seven Terraces and is open to hotel guests and the public. Open daily, 6pm till 10pm. Christopher Ong has worked with the hotel’s chefs to create a menu of a new approach to Classic Straits Chinese Nyonya dishes. They have refined these using French cooking techniques and innovative products to deliver surprising new twists on this already fascinating cuisine. The effect is simple, delicious full of flavor and dazzling. A four-course degustation menu of entrée, mains, vegetables and dessert is served in Chinese 'family-style' to allow guests to share the dishes.

Price range: RM 66.00 – RM 153.00

Cuisines: Chinese, Asian, Fusion, Nonya/Malaysian, Malaysian

5. Reggae Cafe Penang

Reggae Cafe is a pork-free cafe and restaurant offering over 80 cake selections and a variety of non-alcoholic beverages.

Price range: RM 40.00 – RM 60.00

Cuisines: Italian, Cafe, Asian, Malaysian

6. Beach St. Bistrot

Beach St Bistro (BsB) by David & Brian Chin offers modern French, Spanish, and Italian cuisine in a refurbished 100-year-old Georgetown shophouse. Enjoy gourmet pies, burgers, sandwiches, and more in a casual setting with three distinct rooms. Perfect for dining out, lunch, or intimate gatherings.

Price range: RM 22.00 – RM 250.00

Cuisines: French, Cafe, European, Spanish, Wine Bar

7. Makan Kitchen

All- day dining restaurant overlooking the pool with live, interactive stations showcasing the diversity of Malaysian delicacies – Malay, Chinese, Indian and other local specialties. Private dining options available for families and groups.

Price range: RM 15.00 – RM 138.00

Cuisines: Asian, Malaysian

8. Healy Mac's Irish Bar & Restaurant

Cuisines: Irish, Bar, European, Pub

9. Andrew's Kampung

Cuisines: Chinese, Asian, Nonya, Malaysian

Cuisines: Asian, Nonya/Malaysian, Malaysian
